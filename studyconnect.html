StudyConnect

StudyConnect is a dynamic online education platform designed to bridge the gap between students and expert tutors across a wide range of academic subjects. Whether you're looking for one-on-one tutoring, subject-specific help, or guidance for competitive exams, StudyConnect makes quality education accessible and personalized.

The platform offers features like detailed student and tutor profiles, real-time messaging, session scheduling, subject reviews, coin-based transactions, and instant notifications. Tutors can showcase their expertise, while students can explore, connect, and learn in a secure and user-friendly environment.

Built with the goal of making education more interactive and tailored, StudyConnect empowers learners to take control of their academic journey, anytime, anywhere.
